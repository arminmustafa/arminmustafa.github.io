Biography

I am currently a Research Fellow in the Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing group at the University of Surrey, UK. I finished PhD in Computer Vision from the University of Surrey (UK), supervised by Prof. Adrian Hilton. My PhD introduced novel methods for general dynamic scene reconstruction from multi-view videos. I have also worked at Samsung Research Institute, Bangalore, India for 3 years in Computer Vision. Previously, in 2010, I recieved my M.Tech. degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, India, where my thesis was supervised by Prof. K.S. Venkatesh on Human Computer Interaction.

My research interests include Computer Vision, mainly related with 3D Reconstruction and Segmentation.
